Helpful Facts about Copyright Law and the Web


Be Aware of Copyrighted Works

All material that has been registed in the United States will have the copyright symbol. The symbol © will affixed directly on the work itself. Before you copy or reproduce anything make sure the work is not protected by copyright.

Cite all Works

If you use any part of a copyrighted work in your research or project it is important that you cite where you got the information from.

Duration of Copyright

In general a work that has been created on or after January 1, 1978. The work endures a term consisting of the life of the author and 70 years after the death of the author.

Copyright Ownership

Copyrights are generally owned by the person or group that created the works of expressions, but there are some exceptions: like if you work for a company and create something for the company then the company owns the copyright.
